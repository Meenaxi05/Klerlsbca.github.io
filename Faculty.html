Testimonials

RLSI BCA is one among the reputed institues imparting finest quality education. The evolution of the institute over the past 19 years has been able to create an enabling environment for learning and development where future technocrats are fostered. Information technology programme is not pure academics rather it is a preparation for career in the technology industry while staying anchored to academics. The pedagogical practice at RLS BCA has evolved over time by the confluence of different modern approaches backed by state-of-the-art infrastructure. We are committed to train each student on the factors of industry readiness through the fine blending of theory and practice by our experienced faculty members an industry mentors thus enriching life each day.



With our BCA program a student can choose Big Data, Cloud Computing as a foundation for their for career which enables them to gain domain knowledge and practice-oriented learning. The curriculum has been prepared with industry requirements in mind and it emphasizes practical, application-oriented learning.



The Training and Placement Cells ensures that the students are trained to guarantee industry desired employability skills in students. There is also an ample of scope in co-curricular and extracurricular activities wherein the students are encouraged to show their talents. All activities on and off the campus are oriented towards igniting the curiosity of the student and imbibing a culture of continuous learning. The College is proud of the achievements of the students in all kinds of sports activities and IT events. With a radical, cutting edge curriculum, infrastructure, teaching methods and industry interface, RLSBCA is focused on creating students for the future
